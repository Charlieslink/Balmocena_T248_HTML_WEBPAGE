How Computers Work

What I have learned

An information processing cycle is a series of steps that a computer takes to receive, process, store, and output data. These steps are input (data input), processing (computations and performing tasks), storage (storing data for future use), and output (displaying processed information). This cycle is continuous and forms the basis of all computer operations. The basic concept of computer security is to protect computer systems and data from unauthorized access, damage and theft, and to ensure that information remains confidential, intact and available whenever needed.
